Questions Before You Book?

Do you require a deposit to book? +

Yes. A deposit may be required to reserve your date and time. The remaining balance is due before or at the session.

What happens if I need to reschedule? +

If you need to reschedule, reach out as soon as possible. We'll work together to find the next available date.

How soon will I receive my photos? +

Delivery time depends on the session type, but most edited galleries are delivered within a reasonable turnaround after the shoot.

What's included in each photography session? +

Each session includes the shoot time, basic direction/posing help, professionally edited images, and a private gallery or delivery link.
